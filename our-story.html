Our Story

Hi, I'm Reshma,
founder of Hue Story.

My passion for fashion began at a young age and led me to pursue a Bachelor of Fashion Technology from St. Teresa's College, Ernakulam. Fashion has always been more than a profession to me—it is a form of creative expression and a lifelong dream.

Although my career journey led me into accounting, my love for fashion, traditional wear, and styling never faded. Inspired by this dream and supported by my family, I founded Hue Story to create a space where women can discover fashion that feels personal, elegant, and uniquely their own.

Our Vision

My vision goes beyond selling clothing. I dream of bringing more customisation and personalisation to fashion, helping every woman express her individuality through pieces that truly reflect her story.

From curated collections to saree pre-pleating, draping, and custom design services, every detail is chosen with love and purpose.

Because fashion isn't just about what you wear—it's about telling your story.
