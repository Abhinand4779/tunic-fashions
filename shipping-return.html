Refund & Returns Policy

Welcome to HUE. We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If for any reason you are not, we're here to help.

Return Conditions

Items must be returned in their original condition and packaging. Returns will only be accepted if:

  • The item is returned within 7 days of delivery.
  • The item is in its original unused condition.
  • The original tags and packaging are intact.

Refund Process

Once we receive your item, we will inspect it and notify you that we have received your returned item. We will immediately notify you on the status of your refund after inspecting the item.

If your return is approved, we will initiate a refund to your original method of payment (or as a credit to your account, based on your preference). You will receive the credit within a certain amount of days, depending on your card issuer's policies.

Items Not Accepted for Return

Certain types of items cannot be returned, like personalized jewelry or items sold during clearance sales.

Contact Us

If you have any questions on how to return your item to us, please reach out at support@astrabash.com.
